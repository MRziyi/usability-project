## Target Product Selection

We chose **Google Looker Studio** because it is a widely used data visualization and reporting tool with a complex editing interface that presents numerous usability challenges.

### Why Looker Studio?

- Large user base with varying levels of technical expertise
- Complex report creation and editing workflows
- Known usability pain points in chart configuration and data source management
- Rich interaction patterns to evaluate against heuristics
